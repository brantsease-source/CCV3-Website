Our Story

A Gold Country Institution Since 1986

Nestled in the heart of California's legendary Gold Country, the Coloma Club Bar & Grill has been a gathering place for locals and adventurers alike for four decades.

Situated near historic Coloma — where James Marshall's discovery sparked the Gold Rush of 1848 — we've built our own legacy of warm hospitality, honest food, and genuine good times on the banks of the American River.

Whether you're fresh off the river after a day of whitewater adventure, exploring the gold fields, or simply looking for a great meal and a cold drink in good company — you'll always find a seat at the Coloma Club.
